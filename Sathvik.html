Team SMR — Sathvik Myla

Age: 16 | Co-Founder & Interface Designer

Hi, my name is Sathvik Myla, and I am a 16-year-old co-founder of Eco Quest, representing team SMR.

Why We Made It: We created this game-style app to build a cleaner community by making environmental action fun and purposeful. By using green leaves (XP) on a leaderboard and offering prizes to the top three finishers, we want to give people a rewarding incentive to clean up surrounding pollution and care for the environment.

My Contributions: As a co-founder, my main job was to create a strong, dependable interface that our users could rely on. I built the app's homepage and designed a smooth, clean user interface. Additionally, I set up and managed our team's GitHub repository where all of our project code is securely saved.
