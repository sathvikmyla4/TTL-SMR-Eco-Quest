## 🌿 Team Member: Sathvik Myla
Role: Co-Founder
Age: 16

Hi, my name is Sathvik Myla, and I am the co-founder of Eco Quest.

We designed this interactive, game-style app to turn environmental cleanup into a fun competition where users earn green leaves (XP) to climb a community leaderboard. To inspire people to clean up local pollution and build a healthier environment, exciting prizes are awarded to the top three participants.

I love this concept because it transforms vital community action into an engaging, rewarding experience for everyone involved!
