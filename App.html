The Goal

Why EcoQuest exists

🌍

Fighting Pollution Through Play

To combat local pollution and boost volunteer activation, Eco Quest provides a thriving community website with an engaging, gamified platform — linking cleanup data into a fun, interactive experience.

Checkout Eco Quest today to clean your local hot spots, and start transforming neglected litter into self-worth cleaned up.

✍️ Meet the Team

The Minds Behind
Eco Quest

Young builders on a mission to make the planet cleaner, one cleanup at a time.

"Our goal is to transform neighborhood cleanups into an engaging, gamified experience that brings communities together — inspiring friendly competition among local friends, schools, and clubs to see who can make the greatest collective impact."
🌐
Sathvik Myla
Co-Founder & Lead Developer
16 y/o
"I wanted to create this app to make boring neighborhood cleanups feel like a fun, exciting game. By adding points and a leaderboard, I wanted to inspire my neighbors to team up and feel proud of keeping our local parks clean."
