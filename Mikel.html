Team Member Mikel Banks

Hello, my name is Mikel Banks, and I am the co-founder of Eco Quest, a game-style app that uses green leaves (XP) on a leaderboard, with prizes given to the top 3 people. It was created to build a clean community while making it fun and providing a purpose. The prizes are to encourage people to participate and clean up the surrounding pollution. I like this app idea because it offers people a fun way to clean up the environment while earning rewards. I am part of SMR, and my contributions to this project included graphic design for the second high-fidelity wireframe model. I also made the website go to different pages from the nav bar instead of jumping to links.
