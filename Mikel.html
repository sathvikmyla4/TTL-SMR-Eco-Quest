Team Member Mikel Banks

Hello, my name is Mikel Banks, and I am the co-founder of Eco Quest, a game-style app that uses green leaves (XP) on a leaderboard, with prizes given to the top 3 people. It was created to build a clean community while making it fun and providing a purpose. The prizes are to encourage people to participate and clean up the surrounding pollution. I like this app idea as it gives people a fun way to clean the environment while giving rewards.
